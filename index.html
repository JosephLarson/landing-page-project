Aminatou, the Fateshifter!

Aminatou the fateshifter magic revolves around fate and manipulation of destiny. The manipulation of destiny can be seen in her first and second abilitys. Allowing you to control the fate of the cards and to change the fate of cards on the field. The last ability is Aminatou manipulating destiny, completly changing the destiny of those around her.


Aminatou is my favorite commander to play, because the gameplay is always diverse and you never know the direction the game will take.
